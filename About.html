Meet the team

Hello! My name is Aadhya and I am a rising sophomore at North Creek high school. In my spare time I like to read, listen to music, play the guitar, and research topics that I find interesting. However, as I grow older, I find that I have less time to take care of myself and do the things I enjoy. I often have so many things to do that I can feel overwhelmed and burnt out. I wanted to make this website to help others like me who struggle with finding a balance between their work and personal life. This website is meant to remind people that sometimes the best way to be productive is to take a break and listen to music, or read a book. I hope this website helps you take time for yourself!
